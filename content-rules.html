Content Rules

All ads and user content must comply with these rules. Violations may result in removal, account restriction, or reporting to authorities.

Prohibited content

  • Illegal activity, including but not limited to: trafficking, exploitation, content involving minors, or any material that violates UK or applicable law.
  • Fraud, scams, or misleading offers.
  • Harassment, threats, or incitement to violence.
  • Impersonation or false identity.
  • Content that infringes intellectual property or other third-party rights.
  • Spam, automated posting, or repeated identical ads in breach of our policies.

Moderation

We use a combination of:

  • Manual approval: Ads may be reviewed by moderators before or after publication.
  • Automated filters: To detect banned keywords, spam, and policy violations.
  • User reports: You can report any ad via the “Report” button. We review reports and remove content that breaks these rules.

Content that is illegal or unsafe will be removed immediately and may be reported to the relevant authorities.

Categories

Ads must be posted in the correct category and must comply with the law and these rules. Adult or illegal content is not allowed.

Reporting

If you see an ad that breaks these rules, use the Report option on the ad or contact us via the Contact page. We take reports seriously and will act where appropriate.
