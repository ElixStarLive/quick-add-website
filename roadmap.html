Growth roadmap

Scale from 0 to 10,000+ job listings. Phases 1–5 with actions and outcomes. Following this, job boards like Indeed or local niche boards grow from a handful of jobs to tens of thousands.

Phase 1 – Seed the marketplace

0 → 500 jobs

Goal: Make your site look active so visitors trust it.

  • Add initial jobs manually – rewrite postings from public job boards or company websites.
  • Offer free posting to employers – especially local small businesses.
  • Reach out to local businesses directly – call/email restaurants, warehouses, cleaning services, delivery companies.
  • Use social media – Facebook/LinkedIn groups for local jobs.
  • Recruiter partnerships – offer bulk posting for free or discounted rates.

✅ Outcome: 400–500 jobs, a mix of manually added and live employer postings.

Phase 2 – Build employer relationships

500 → 2,000 jobs

Goal: Encourage businesses to post regularly.

  • Email marketing – newsletters to employers highlighting free posting or featured ads.
  • Referral program – reward (free featured job or discount) to businesses that refer other employers.
  • Featured listings upsell – start monetizing while incentivizing quality postings.
  • Local networking events – job fairs or business meetups to get companies on board.

✅ Outcome: 1,500–2,000 listings, mostly from active employers who post regularly.

Phase 3 – Automate job inflows

2,000 → 5,000 jobs

Goal: Reduce manual work and increase volume.

  • Import jobs via RSS feeds – many companies publish jobs on their websites.
  • Scrape public job boards (legally) – pull jobs from open APIs or sites with permission.
  • Recruitment agency feeds – partner with agencies to get their open positions.
  • Category expansion – add more industries to increase posting volume.

✅ Outcome: Thousands of jobs flowing automatically each week, scaling your listings.

Phase 4 – Incentivize large employers

5,000 → 10,000 jobs

Goal: Focus on high-volume employers and agencies.

  • Bulk posting packages – discounts for posting 50–100+ jobs at a time.
  • Subscription model – monthly fees for unlimited postings in certain categories.
  • Premium visibility – featured jobs, top placements, promoted email blasts.
  • Partner with universities and job centers – internships, part-time, graduate jobs in bulk.

✅ Outcome: 10,000+ listings, mostly from active, paying employers and agencies.

Phase 5 – Maintain quality & trust

Ongoing

Goal: Prevent spam, fake jobs, and user churn.

  • Manual or AI moderation of job posts.
  • Reporting system for users (e.g. Report this job).
  • Verify employer accounts.
  • Regularly remove expired listings.

Extra tips for rapid scaling

  • Social proof: Show numbers like “3,000+ jobs posted this month” to encourage posting.
  • Hot niches first: Start with industries that hire often – warehouses, delivery, cleaning.
  • Analytics: Track which categories and sources bring the most jobs.
  • Automate reminders: Email employers whose jobs are about to expire to repost.
